Become a Multi-faceted Future Ready Engineer

An undergraduate engineering degree programme B.Tech (Bachelor of Technology) in saraswati college ,is a 4 years full time programme in a specific branch of engineering (i.e. Major degree). Students are required to earn approximately 160 credits spread over these 4 years in any one of the following Major programmes

  • Computer Engineering
  • Electronics Engineering
  • Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering
  • Information Technology
  • Mechanical Engineering

These undergraduate B.Tech programmes are designed to acquire knowledge and skills, provide a base for lifelong learning and develop an attitude for adapting to technological advances. The curriculum is a blend of courses in basic and engineering sciences, branch specific courses and a wide variety of electives ranging from Technical / Humanities and Social Sciences / Foreign Languages / Management. Internships in Industry / Academic & Research Institutions are also encouraged.

Who can apply?

  • An Indian student who has passed 10+2 examination in Science with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects
  • NRI / Foreign Nationals / Children of Gulf workers/ OCI students

Requirements

  • A student who has Passed HSC or its equivalent examination with 50% marks in requisite subjects
  • Non zero score in Entrance exams such as MHT-CET/JEE-Mains (Paper-1) / SAT (Scholastic Assessment Test) Exam etc.

Why pursue an Undergraduate Programme in saraswati college?

  • Curriculum designed and revised in collaboration with experts from Industry and Academia.
  • Exposure to the outside world through vibrant student activities and competitions.
  • Excellent ambience and infrastructure for holistic development
  • Opportunity to choose from multiple career paths
  • Transform yourself into a competent Engineer
